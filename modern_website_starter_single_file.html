Our Story

For over two decades, Union Cigarettes has maintained a steadfast commitment to quality, precision, and integrity in tobacco manufacturing. Founded in Dubai in 2004, we serve regional and international markets with consistency and care from our Jebel Ali Free Zone facility.

Vision, Mission & Values

Vision: To set the standard for modern cigarette manufacturing—where craftsmanship, compliance, and consistency define every product.

Mission: Uphold the highest standards through advanced systems, rigorous quality controls, and a culture built on consistency, compliance, and trust.

  • Excellence • Superior quality in products and processes.
  • Integrity • Honesty and transparency.
  • Innovation • Continuous improvement.
  • Collaboration • Strong relationships inside and out.
  • Responsibility • Respect for stakeholders, environment, and communities.
